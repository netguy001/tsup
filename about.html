Our story

Who we are

Techscale is a technology solutions company based in India, focused on helping businesses build and scale reliable infrastructure. We work with startups and enterprises that need engineering expertise to support their growth.

Our approach combines technical depth with practical execution. Whether you're building your first product or modernizing existing systems, we provide the architecture, automation, and infrastructure capabilities that let you focus on your business.

Founded on the principle that every business deserves access to world-class infrastructure—we've helped companies from early-stage startups to enterprises handling millions of users build systems that scale.

We started by helping early-stage startups build their first products. Today, we work with businesses across industries, providing the technical foundation they need to grow without constantly firefighting infrastructure issues. Our team brings deep expertise in cloud architecture, automation, AI implementation, and enterprise-grade system design.
